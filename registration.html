The registration website is live now. During the registration, there will be an option to request visa letter. If you have any questions regarding registration and visa letter, please contact our Registration Chair Dr. Shuhui Yang at shuhuiyang@pnw.edu.

REGISTRATION POLICY

  • Author registration deadline for conference is August 15, 2025, for all workshops is August 22, 2025.
  • At least one author of an accepted conference/workshop paper must register at the conference/workshop non-student rate by deadlines. An author with multiple accepted conference paper can have the discounted rate for any additional paper. The registered author must attend and present the paper. If more than one author attends, each author must register separately. If none of the authors can attend, they must find a proxy to present their paper in person.
  • Member registration rates are available for professional members of IEEE.
  • The page limits are 9 pages for the main-conference regular papers, 7 pages for short papers, 6 pages for workshop papers, and 2 pages for posters. For all categories of publications, one extra page can be purchased at a cost of $150. Only one extra page is allowed.
  • Registration can be canceled on or before September 15. 2025. There will be an administrative fee of $150 deducted from each refund.
  • For registration questions, please contact our Registration Chair Dr. Shuhui Yang at shuhuiyang@pnw.edu.
