Venue

VENUE INFORMATION

Conviser Law Center (Chicago‑Kent College of Law), part of the Illinois Institute of Technology and located at 565 West Adams Street in downtown Chicago's West Loop, offers an ideal venue for conferences thanks to its central, easily accessible location near the financial and legal district, and excellent transit connections. It sits just blocks west of the Chicago River and the scenic Riverwalk, offering attendees easy access to waterfront dining, public art, boat and kayak rentals, and open-air green space alongside the river. Located adjacent to the Loop, the city’s central business district, the venue is within walking distance of major corporate headquarters, cultural landmarks, theaters, and renowned restaurants. It benefits from outstanding connectivity—just one block west of Union Station and three blocks south of Ogilvie Transportation Center, with nearby CTA transit access and highways I‑90/94 and I‑290 close by. This prime urban location ensures attendees can seamlessly combine conference programming with scenic riverfront strolls, world‑class dining, and convenient access to the heart of downtown Chicago. For more information, visit Chicago‑Kent College of Law, Illinois Tech.

VENUE: Conviser Law Center (Chicago‑Kent College of Law) - Illinois Institute of Technology

ADDRESS: Conviser Law Center, 565 W Adams St, Chicago, IL 60661

Getting Around: Chicago-Kent College of Law is located in Chicago's West Loop neighborhood, one block west of Union Station (Amtrak and Metra commuter trains) and three blocks south of the Ogilvie Transportation Center (Chicago/Northwestern station for Metra commuter trains). Both train stations are the terminus of numerous Chicago Transit Authority (CTA) bus routes, making it easy to catch a cab, train, or bus to or from practically anywhere in the city or suburbs. For those that drive, the law school's proximity to Chicago's major north/south and east/west expressways—just two blocks east of the Kennedy Expressway (I-90/94) and three blocks north of the Eisenhower Expressway (I-290)—makes commuting easy. Check out the CTA's system maps and trip planner at transitchicago.com.
